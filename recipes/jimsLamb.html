Jim's Lamb

Description

This is Jim's scary lamb

Ingredients

  • ½ cup lemon juice
  • ½ cup orange juice
  • ¼ cup Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons dry mustard
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon liquid smoke flavoring
  • ½ teaspoon lemon pepper

Steps

  1. Combine lemon juice, orange juice, Worcestershire sauce, olive oil, oregano, honey, garlic, mustard, garlic powder, liquid smoke, and lemon pepper in a bowl.
